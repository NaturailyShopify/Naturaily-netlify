05 Process

Develop your product
fast and stress-free

At Naturaily, we use the combination of Scrum and Kanban frameworks to deliver digital products that generate value to companies and users.

Our agile development teams aim to take your project to market in the shortest time possible - testing, iterating and, ultimately, developing a scalable product.

Our specialists will take good care of you

  • 01

    Product Owner
    /The Client

    The business owner addresses the priorities, as well as expresses the needs and problems.

  • 02

    Project
    Manager

    This person keeps everything on track: projects, tasks, budget, time and even developers.
    They will be your first contact on an everyday basis, asking you questions, arranging meetings and presenting the project’s progress.

  • 03

    Development
    Team

    Software developers, QA specialists and any other experts that may be needed.
    They will deliver functionalities, develop the system and test it, all while cooperating with the PM and the design team.

  • 04

    Design
    Team

    UX and UI specialists will plan your product’s architecture, flow and illustrations, as well as create beautiful interfaces for your users.

You will be up to date with our proven work and communication “Scrumban” process

  • 01

    Sprint

    The Scrum element of our process. Usually, we work in 2-week sprints. Each sprint has a goal to deliver some piece of functionality at the end of the iteration. When the development work is over for the sprint, the team spends together approximately 2 hours on planning the next steps.

  • 02

    Kanban

    The Kanban board always reflects the current state of the development process. From To-Do to On Stage, developers are responsible for managing their tasks. After the branch is merged to development, it is the responsibility of the QA and PM to see if everything works as it should, as well as schedule and confirm deployment on the production environment.

  • 03

    Slack

    If any obstacles appear, or some requirements need to be precise, we will immediately alert you about it on the Slack channel. This is also where information about the current state of the project is shared among the team members.
