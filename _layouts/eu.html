Naturaily is implementing the project Intensification of export activities of Naturaily Sp. z o. o. on foreign markets within the sub-measure 3.3.3. SME SUPPORT IN PROMOTION OF PRODUCT BRANDS - GO TO BRAND The Intelligent Development Operational Program co-financed from the European Regional Development Fund.
About the project

What it's all about...

The goal of the project is performing activities aimed at intensifying the export operations of Naturaily on foreign markets, focusing on promising markets and the European Union. Market analysis and global trends indicate that the services planned to be promoted will allow the company to become recognizable on selected promising and EU markets.

Naturaily will take part in 6 trade and exhibition events in Israel, Japan, USA, Portugal and Finland as part of the project. Participation of Naturaily representatives in the IT / ICT industry promotion program will allow to promote IT services, in particular in the field of digital transformation.

The implementation of planned activities is expected to allow for the presence on foreign markets, including those not yet serviced, which will increase the number of clients served and will be reflected in the growing revenues of the company. Participation in fairs and meetings with potential partners will allow us to better understand clients' needs and gain a strong position on foreign markets.
